BxMCTF 2023

Tue, 30 May 2023, 04:00 UTC — Sat, 03 June 2023, 03:59 UTC 

On-line

A WxMCTF event.

Format: Jeopardy 

Official URL: https://ctfmgci.jonathanw.dev/

This event's future weight is subject of public voting!

Future weight: 23.59 

Rating weight: 23.59 

Event organizers 
  • wxmctf

Spanning from May 30th at 00:00 EDT to June 3rd at 23:59 EDT, we are excited to announce that MGCI and BSS have joined together to host the BxMCTF cybersecurity competition with over $20k in prizes!

To participate, create an account and then enter the contest from the Contests page when the contest starts. If participating in a team, please register one account for the whole team. For Ontario high school students, please register with your school email.

For any questions, join our community Discord.
